### Andrology is a Key to Male Health

**Andrology** is a modern medical specialty dealing with male reproductive system disorders both congenital and acquired, including the following pathology:

- сongenital constitution of genital organs;
- acquired deformity of genitalia;
- inappropriate urination;
- inflammatory and infectious diseases of urogenital apparatus;
- erectile and ejaculation disorders;
- neoplasms of various nature;
- gender identity issues;
- plastic and reconstructive surgery.

Andrology is a symbiosis of the following areas: urology, immunology, endocrinology, surgery and [reproductive medicine](http://irm.kz/tsentr-eko/). Therefore, an **andrologist** can resolve a wide range of issues related to male reproductive health.

### Urologist and Andrologist Advise in RMI’s Andrology Clinic (Almaty)

**Advise of a urologist and an andrologist** is necessary in case of the following symptoms:

- painful condition in the area of genitalia or lower abdomen;
- pruritis, burning, abnormal discharges from urethra;
- inappropriate urination, urine discoloration;
- inclusions (purulence, blood, etc.) in urine or sperm;
- decreased libido or alibidimia;
- [erectile dysfunction](http://irm.kz/andrology-2/erektilnaya-disfunktsiya-lechenie-impotentsii/) and soreness during sexual intercourse;
- rash, irritation and erosion in the area of genitalia;
- tumors of urinary or reproductive systems;
- sexual development disorders or arrest;
- female secondary sexual characteristics in kids and males, etc.;
- genitals trauma.

Infertility treatment and male reproductive health recovery is the main activity of RMI’s [Andrology Department](http://irm.kz/andrology-2/otdelenie/). Our clinic uses the latest assisted reproductive technologies and best practices developed by RMI’s experts. This allows coping with complex and substandard cases and to return fulfilling life to our patients.
As a rule an expert visit includes the following phases:

- collection of data regarding hereditary and previous diseases, surgeries, bad health habits, etc.;
- examination and palpation of genitalia for obvious defects, inflammations and neoplasms;
- studies to identify patient’s sex constitution;
- clinical and biochemical tests, spermogram;
- ultrasound scanning and Doppler sonography (if necessary);
- other experts advice: general practitioner, endocrinologist, cardiologist, etc..

Medications or surgery is prescribed based upon the results of tests and visual examination. All prescriptions take into the account individual peculiarities of the patient’s body, his age and health condition.
